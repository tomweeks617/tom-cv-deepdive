# Tom Outside of Work

Away from the keyboard, Tom's life is a happily busy one. He lives in a town in Cambridgeshire that is comfortably commutable to London, and he has two young children who keep him on his toes (and account for most of his free time, in the best way).

When he does carve out time for himself, it's usually on a pitch or a fairway: he plays 6-a-side football every week, and he loves getting out on the golf course whenever family life allows, which is less often than he'd like. His golf handicap is strictly confidential. Not for privacy reasons, just self-preservation: he is, by his own admission, much better at analytics than golf.

If asked what Tom is like as a person or colleague, it's fair to say he brings the same energy to work that weekly football and two small children demand: team-oriented, quick on his feet, and good-humoured under pressure.
